Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

OneTime ("we", "our", or "us") operates the OneTime mobile application and web application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and phone number when you create an account.
  • Authentication Data: We support sign-in via email/password, Google, and Apple Sign-In. We receive your name and email from these providers but never your passwords from third-party providers.
  • Job Data: Job cards, schedules, client information, notes, and other business data you enter into the Service.
  • Photos: Images you upload for job documentation and receipts, stored securely in our cloud storage.
  • Organisation Data: Business name, address, contact details, tax information, and team member information.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To manage your account and organisation
  • To enable job card creation, scheduling, and team management
  • To generate PDF job cards and reports
  • To send you important service notifications
  • To improve and develop the Service

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides enterprise-grade security with row-level security policies, encrypted data storage, and secure authentication. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and file storage
  • Google Maps API: Address autocomplete and location services
  • Apple Sign-In: Authentication on iOS devices
  • Google Sign-In: Authentication across platforms

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data as necessary to provide the Service (e.g., with the third-party services listed above).

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated personal data, job cards, and organisation data (if you are the sole owner) are permanently deleted.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the app
  • Update your profile information at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data through the app's Profile settings
  • Export your job cards as PDF documents

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
